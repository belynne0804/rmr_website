About Us


Reg Gibbs MS, CRC, LCPC, CBIS, CLCP, FIALCP

Mr. Gibbs is a vocational rehabilitation counselor, life care planner, expert witness, and medical case manager. He has been the owner of Rocky Mountain Rehab, P.C. for 20 years and is actively involved in continued education as a presenter and participant in the rehabilitation community. He has also served in various roles as a faculty member for both his local university but also to a life care planning training program.
